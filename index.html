My projects

Logistics Application

A web based freight management software, designed for the Freight Forwarders, Transporters, NVOCC, IATA Cargo Agents, Courier and Logistics service providers to efficiently organize, manage and monitor their business. This application capture information from initial sales, quotation, booking and shipment details with auto email & status update mail to various internal andexternal stake holders involved in the life cycle of the shipment.

SYNC

SYNC(Spot Your Next Customer) is a cloud based application, where in it is used to push advertisement directly from our application to remotely played screens. The application is intergrated with online image to video converter, analytics which gives the count(advertisement played), proof of play, device log, acknowledgement details etc.

Faith App

The Faith app is proof that we live in a blessed world. It's our endeavour to bring the faithful in the world closer to their faith by using this app. The app helps unify the faithful of a parish or church enabling a richer means of communication, cooperation and concern. Users can now access information pertaining to their own Parish Church or their favourite member Churches. The app also provides on-demand access to announcements, daily readings, prayers, calendar and Internet radio.

GIEOM Mobility App

The GIEOM software is a comprehensive Business Excellence tool which allows businesses to manage changes to their Business Paths, Regulations & Policies, Skills & Competencies and Business Performance. It enables employees monitor progress using personalized Business Operations analytics generated intelligently by the software in their area of responsibility and create targeted actions for course correction and interventions.

CCE+ (Desktop & Web Application)

This software is developed based on ‘Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation (CCE)’. It is a new system of education introduced by Central Board of Secondary Education in India. It is designed especially for the students of class 1 to 10. The system focuses on evaluating the students both in scholastic and non-scholastic areas. This system is now implemented in Karnataka State by the Karnataka educational department. CCE+ is developed for meeting all the requirements for CCE.
